# Maintenance & Growth Retainers

The goal of a retainer is to ensure the website stays healthy, fast, and competitive while providing steady MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue).

| Package | Name | Services | Monthly Price |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bronze** | **Shield** | Hosting, Security, Backups, 1hr Updates | $150 |
| **Silver** | **Scale** | Includes Shield + SEO, Performance, 4hr Updates | $500 |
| **Gold** | **Engine** | Includes Scale + CRO, Content, Strategy, 10hr Updates | $1,500+ |

## RETAINER WORKFLOW

### 1. The Monthly Result Report (`/wsa:results`)
Every retainer client gets a report by the 5th of each month.
- **Traffic**: vs Previous Period.
- **Conversions**: vs Goal.
- **Heath**: Speed Score, Security Log.
- **Action Plan**: What we do next month to improve.

### 2. Quarterly Strategy Call
Review KPIs. Cross-sell new features (v2 requirements from `REQUIREMENTS.md`).

## SALES PITCH
"You wouldn't buy a Ferrari and never change the oil. Your website is your high-performance business engine. My retainer keeps it running at peak performance so you don't lose customers to slow load times or downtime."
