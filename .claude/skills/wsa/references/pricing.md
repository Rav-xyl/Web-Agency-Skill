## PRICING CALCULATORS

### 1. The Aura Multiplier (TAM)
Adjust base price based on project complexity and deadline.
- **Urgent (< 7 days)**: 2.0x
- **Standard (14-21 days)**: 1.0x
- **Complex Logic**: +$500 per integration
- **Custom Graphics**: +$200 per custom element

### 2. Value-Refine Methodology
Instead of "How many hours?", ask "What is one new customer worth to you?"
- If 1 customer = $1,000 revenue.
- If website brings 10 customers/month = $120,000/year.
- Pricing at $12,000 (10% of added value) is an easy sell.

## PROPOSAL STAGES
1. **Discovery (Paid/Free)**: $250 audit or free pitch.
2. **Deposit**: 50% upfront.
3. **Milestone 1**: 25% after Design Sign-off (Phase 2).
4. **Final**: 25% before Go-Live (Phase 8).
