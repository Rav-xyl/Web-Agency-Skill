## Contract — Minimal Viable Version

```
## Website Services Agreement
Developer: [Your Name] | Client: [Client Name] | Date: [date]

1. SERVICES: Developer will provide services per Project Brief dated [date].
   Scope changes require written Change Request signed by both parties.

2. PAYMENT: Total ₹[X]. Deposit ₹[X] on signing (non-refundable).
   Balance ₹[X] due before launch/credential transfer. Late payment: 2%/month.

3. REVISIONS: [2] rounds included. Additional: ₹[X]/hour.
   A revision round = consolidated list submitted at one time.

4. TIMELINE: [X] weeks from deposit + full content delivery.
   Client delays in providing content extend timeline accordingly.

5. IP: All work becomes Client's property upon final payment in full.
   Prior to final payment, all work remains Developer's property.

6. KILL FEE: <25% complete: deposit forfeited. >25%: 50% of total.
   >50%: 75% of total.

7. WARRANTY: 30 days post-launch bug fix included.
   Excludes new features, client modifications, third-party issues.
   Post-warranty: ₹[X]/hour.

8. LIABILITY: Developer's total liability ≤ fees paid for this project.

9. CLIENT DUTIES: Provide all content and approvals on time.
   Client warrants they have rights to all content provided.

Developer: __________________ Date: __________
Client:    __________________ Date: __________
```
